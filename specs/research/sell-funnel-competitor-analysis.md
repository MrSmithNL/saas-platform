| Leadpages    | Not a true funnel builder (pages only), AI credits run out |

| Systeme.io   | Dated editor, pages look generic, no AI                    |
| Carrd        | Single-page only, no funnels/analytics/A/B                 |

---

## Market Gaps (Our Opportunities)

1. **Affordable AI-native funnel building** — Upper-right quadrant (cheap + high AI) is empty
2. **AI A/B optimization under $100/mo** — Only Unbounce offers this at $249+
3. **Plain-English analytics** — Nobody provides "your checkout page loses 40% because X"
4. **AI conversion coaching** — Proactive suggestions, not just dashboards
5. **Mobile-first creation AND consumption** — Only Perspective does this
6. **One-click funnel cloning with AI adaptation** — "Take this coaching funnel, adapt for yoga studio"
