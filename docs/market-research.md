## Market Growth Drivers

1. **AI integration demand** — 69% of SaaS founders already use AI; customers expect AI-powered features as standard
2. **Small business digital adoption** — Post-COVID acceleration of online sales continues
3. **Creator economy growth** — Solopreneurs, coaches, course creators need simple funnel tools
4. **Fatigue with complex tools** — ClickFunnels, HubSpot are powerful but overwhelming for small businesses
5. **Subscription economy** — Businesses increasingly comfortable with monthly SaaS subscriptions

---

## Key Trends

1. **AI-first funnel generation** — Emerging trend: generate entire funnels from a product description
2. **No-code/low-code** — Drag-and-drop builders are table stakes
3. **Consolidation** — Customers prefer fewer, more integrated tools
4. **Privacy-first analytics** — Cookie consent complexity drives demand for first-party analytics
5. **Mobile optimisation** — All funnels must be mobile-first
