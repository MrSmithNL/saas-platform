**Purpose:** Systematically build the external authority signals that drive 70% of AI citations. On-page SEO and AISO optimization can only take you so far — 77% of AI citations come from sources you don't control (48% earned media, 30% commercial/review content). This capability fills that gap.

**Problem it solves:** Hairgenetix scores 9/10 on technical SEO but 0% SoM on ChatGPT and only 20% on Gemini. The site is invisible to AI because it has zero external authority footprint. No Reddit mentions, no Wikipedia presence, no review platform profiles, no YouTube content, no press coverage, no directory listings.

---

## How It Works — The Authority Stack

External authority for AI visibility is built across 5 layers, each feeding different AI engines:

| Layer                         | What                                                           | Why                                             | Which AI Engines                                       |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **1. Entity Establishment**   | Create the brand as a recognised entity in knowledge databases | AI models recognise entities, not just websites | All (especially ChatGPT, Claude)                       |
| **2. Review & Trust Signals** | Build presence on review platforms                             | 3x higher citation chance with review profiles  | Perplexity, Google AI                                  |
| **3. Community Presence**     | Organic participation in relevant communities                  | Reddit = 6.6% of Perplexity citations           | Perplexity, Gemini                                     |
| **4. Content Distribution**   | Publish on external platforms (YouTube, Medium, guest posts)   | YouTube = #1 Google AI source                   | Google AI Overviews, Copilot                           |
| **5. Earned Media & PR**      | Press coverage, directory listings, Wikipedia                  | 48% of all AI citations come from earned media  | ChatGPT (Wikipedia = 7.8%), Claude (directories = 65%) |

## Implementation: Claude's Role

### Automated Monthly Cycle

Each month, Claude runs this cycle:

1. **SoM measurement** — test 10 queries on ChatGPT + Gemini, record results
2. **Authority audit** — check which platforms have active profiles, what's missing
3. **Content drafting** — produce next month's external content (Reddit drafts, Medium articles, guest post pitches, press materials)
4. **Competitor tracking** — check competitor SoM, note what they're doing that works
5. **Progress report** — update tracker with new scores and actions taken

### Content Templates (Claude Produces)

| Template               | Format                                        | Frequency              |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ---------------------- |
